Welcome to Flower Finder!

Discover the perfect flower to plant in your garden based on your zodiac sign!

How Flower Finder works:

  • Pick your birthdate and click the “Find My Flower” button to see what flower is best planted based on the time of year your birthday falls in.
  • Click any flower icon to see more details about that flower. Each card shows the flower's name, care levels (difficulty, sunlight, watering), description, and a fun fact.
  • Use the speaker icon to play the flower's unique sound.
  • Use the back arrow to return to the grid.
